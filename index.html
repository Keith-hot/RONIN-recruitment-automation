The Challenge of Hard-to-Reach Audiences

B2B market research firms like RONIN International face a universal paradox: the most valuable insights come from the hardest people to reach.

C-Suite Executives

Doctors, CTOs, and CISOs receive hundreds of outreach emails monthly. Generic invites are ignored.

High Acquisition Cost

Manual research, drafting, and follow-up consume 15+ minutes per lead. At scale, this becomes unsustainable.

Follow-Up Gaps

80% of conversions require 5+ touchpoints, yet most teams stop after 1–2 attempts. Warm leads go cold.

5-Module Smart Architecture

An end-to-end pipeline that scores, personalizes, nurtures, and triggers multi-channel outreach—automatically.

1. Data Layer

Respondent profiles + 5,000 interaction records

2. Lead Scoring

LightGBM classifier (AUC 0.93) with stratified weights

3. Personalization

RAG engine with FAISS + Sentence-Transformers

4. Sequences

7-state nurture machine with time-based triggers

5. Triggers

Event-driven rules across Email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp
